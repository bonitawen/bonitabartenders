About Us

Bonita Bartenders lead by Wendy specializes in private events throughout the Los Angeles area. Bonita Bartenders can make classic or signature drinks. All our ingredients are sourced from natural ingredients. We handcraft your drinks according to your taste. We offer a professional courteous staff for you and your guests. Included in our services is a special drink menu that can be customized to suit you. Bonita Bartenders goal is to ensure that your event runs smoothly and efficiently!!

About Wendy

Wendy is a happy, energetic mixologist. Her bartending career started in NYC years ago. After a successful opportunity leading a nightclub in Hollywood, CA, she decided to follow her love for bartending, creating Bonita Bartenders in 2014. Wendys mission is to provide an impeccable service, great drinks and an amazing experience!
